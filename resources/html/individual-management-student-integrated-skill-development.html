Individual - Management Student - Integrated Skills Development (ISD) Program

This program is designed for the Non-IT Aspirants (pursuing BE/BTech programs) who are looking for a strategic approach to develop Behavioral Skills. The long term learning intervention used in this program would help students develop strong profile by the time they get ready to appear in the Campus Recruitment Process of the companies. The road-map for this program provides direction to the students about the optimum timing and order for completing different training programs so that they can make most out of them and meet their final objective of starting a career with their dream company.
