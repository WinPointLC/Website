Case Study -

?Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) @ Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT)

Successfully completed an engagement with PICT including design, development, and delivery of CRT program. This strategic program was targeted for Niche IT Organizations including Product Companies and Technology Start-ups. The objective of this program was to develop skilled people for niche companies in IT Industry through finishing school/boot camp training. The participants of this program were third/fourth year engineering students of Computer and IT. The CRT program was delivered in 2 waves - 12th February 2015 to 31st March 2015 (Wave I) and 26th June 2015 to 31st July 2015 (Wave II).

Pioneered CRT for Niche IT Organizations (Product Companies and Technology Start-ups) which are highly selective in their hiring of fresh engineering graduates. PICT engaged us for rolling out this program as they were looking to improve the metric of placement of their students with niche companies.

Outcome driven program ensuring measurable impact on the participants:
  1. 50 to 60% of the students placed as of 31st July 2015 were participants in the CRT program
  2. On a scale of '1 to 5' customer feedback indicated was at an average of 4.7
Value Additions:
  1. Transferred knowledge on the ?Profiles of the visiting companies? and it?s ?Evaluation Process?
  2. Mock Aptitude Tests, Mock GD sessions, Mock HRI, Mock Coding Assignments
  3. ?Resume? and ?Self-Introduction Script? Review and Personal Feedback
  4. Mentoring for Career Development
  5. Guidance for Discovery/Due Diligence for the target compan
Program Outline:
  1. Technical & General Aptitude
  2. Coding
  3. Technical and Behavioral Interview
  4. Group Discussion
Program Structure (90 Hrs):
  1. Technical Evaluation (Technical Aptitude + Coding + Technical Interview) - 56 Hrs
    - C Programming, OOP/C++ Programming, Java Programming, Data Structures, Databases, Operating System, and Computer Networks
  2. General Aptitude - 24 Hrs
    - Quantitative Ability, Verbal Ability, and Logical Reasoning
  3. Group Discussion - 6 Hrs
    - Group Task and Group Discussion
  4. HR Interview - 4 Hrs
    - Management Interview and HR Interview
