Technology Institute
(IT stream)

Target Audience

The institutes which offer Information Technology Programs:

  • The institutes offering mainstream programs such as BE/BTech/ME/MTech (Comp and IT) and MCA
  • Other institutes offering any undergraduate/postgraduate/diploma program for the students who have an aptitude and interest to explore a technology career in the IT industry.

To perform any role effectively and efficiently, you need a strong blend of hard skills and soft skills (e.g. for software developer, coding is a hard skill and communication is a soft skill). WinPoint believes in Role-based Competency Development where we focus on the niche area of Product/Application Development and Maintenance for development of hard skill (i.e. software skills).

Since the IT aspirants are quite busy with their regular academic activities, the Technology Institutes (IT stream) should just focus on the development of key professional skills of their students (for their stage in the career life-cycle) . Given the evaluation process requirements of IT companies, Technology Institutes (IT stream) are advised to focus on the following:

  • Software Module
  • Behavioral Module
  • Integrated Skills Development (ISD) Program
  • Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) Program
  • Faculty Development Program
